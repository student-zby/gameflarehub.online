About the Game

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced, wave-survival action game. Navigate swarms of enemies, collect experience orbs, stack powerful upgrades, and craft unstoppable builds. Each run is short, intense, and endlessly replayable.

How to Play

  • Move to avoid enemies and collect experience.
  • Choose upgrades between waves to power up your build.
  • Survive as long as possible to set a new high score.

Core Features

  • Instant play in browser (no download)
  • Mobile-friendly and PC-optimized
  • Rogue-lite upgrades and build variety
  • Short sessions with high replay value

Controls

Desktop

  • WASD or Arrow keys to move
  • Mouse for menus and selections

Mobile

  • On-screen touch joystick and buttons
  • Tap to confirm upgrades
